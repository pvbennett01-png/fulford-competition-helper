Configure competition & prize fund

Competition details

Division boundaries

Defaults are editable. Clicking “Parse divisions” recalculates using the current boundaries.

Entry fee & retention

Yes: 75% of entry fee per player available as prize fund (25% retained).
No: 100% of entry fee available as prize fund.

Two's prize funds

Fund ÷ total shares. Each "2" = 1 share, each "1" (hole-in-one) = 2 shares. Multiple twos on different holes earn multiple shares.

Prize limits

The engine auto-calculates prize places to exhaust the fund between these limits. Prizes taper linearly from max down to min.
