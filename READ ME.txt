FULFORD COMPETITION HELPER
==========================
Licensed to Fulford Golf Club — not for distribution.

HOW TO INSTALL
--------------
1. Extract this ZIP folder to your Desktop
   (Right-click the ZIP → "Extract All" → choose Desktop → Extract)

2. Open the "Fulford Competition Helper" folder on your Desktop

3. Double-click "Prize_Fund_Calc_V13.html" to open the app in your browser

TIP: Right-click "Prize_Fund_Calc_V13.html" → Send to → Desktop (create shortcut)
     to add a quick-launch shortcut to your Desktop.

DEMO DATA
---------
The "demo-data" folder contains sample medal.csv and stableford.csv files
you can use to try out the import feature.

SUPPORT
-------
Contact your competition committee secretary for assistance.
